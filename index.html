01 / About

A profile shaped between two disciplines.

I chose mechatronics for its scope: circuits, mechanics, software, and industrial automation all under one discipline. Throughout my degree at Tecnológico de Monterrey I worked on industry-linked projects with ABB, Schneider Electric, John Deere, Carrier, and MIT. That hands-on exposure helped me see where my interests really sat.

One of those interests was data. The industrial projects I worked on generated plenty of it, and the analysis side pulled me in. With the rise of large language models and the broader AI wave, the MSc in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at Hull felt like the obvious next step.

So I work the seam between both. Production-grade work, ROI math included.
