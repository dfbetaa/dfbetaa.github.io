01 / About

A profile shaped between two disciplines.

I started in mechatronics for one reason: I wanted to build systems that work in the real world. Robots that pack bottles, vehicles that navigate without a driver, vision systems that catch defects a tired inspector would miss.

The MSc in Data Science & AI at Hull came from a parallel observation. The same factories I was automating were generating more data than they could use. Industrial AI isn't a buzzword — it's the next logical layer on top of every PLC and cobot I've programmed.

So I work the seam between both. Production-grade work, ROI math included.
